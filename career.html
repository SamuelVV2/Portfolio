Software engineer

What do they do?

A Software Engineer is an IT professional who designs, develops and maintains computer software at a company. They use their creativity and technical skills and apply the principles of software engineering to help solve new and ongoing problems for an organization. They do this alongside other coworkers, in which communitaion is needed.
